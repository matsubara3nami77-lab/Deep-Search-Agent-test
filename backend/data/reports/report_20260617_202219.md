This decentralized approach to power generation not only insulates countries from global price shocks but also reduces the risk of conflict over resource depletion and control.\n\n## Conclusion\n\nThe transition to renewable energy is no longer a peripheral environmental goal; it is a central pillar of global economic and geopolitical strategy.
